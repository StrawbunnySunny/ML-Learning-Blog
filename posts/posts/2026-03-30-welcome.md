# Welcome to My ML Journal!

So I decided to start a journal to track my machine learning progress. It won't be anything formal or serious, it is just for fun! It'd be really cool if I could become good at ML, I just find it to be a very intriguing subject so far (with my limited knowledge...). 

## Why a journal?

A few reasons:

- **Accountability**: If I write down what I'm learning, I'm more likely to actually stick with it.
- **Understanding**: Explaining things in my own words forces me to actually understand them (or realize that I don't).
- **Reference**: Future me will thank present me for writing things down instead of trying to remember that one concept I learned three weeks ago.
- **Aesthetic**: Making this journal provides me with an excuse to create a new pink, cute and sparkly website.
