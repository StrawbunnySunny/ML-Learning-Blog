# Welcome to My ML Journal!

So I decided to start a journal to track my machine learning progress. It's just a fun way for me to write down new things I've learned and places where I'm totally lost. 

## Why a journal?

A few reasons:

- **Accountability**: If I write down what I'm learning, I'm more likely to actually stick with it.
- **Understanding**: Explaining things in my own words forces me to actually understand them (or realize that I don't).
- **Reference**: Future me will thank present me for writing things down instead of trying to remember that one concept I learned three weeks ago.
- **Aesthetic**: This is the perfect excuse for me to create a pink, sparkly and adorable website.
